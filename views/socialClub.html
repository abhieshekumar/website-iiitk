Trendles Club

(Social Club)

Introduction

For the smooth working of any institution there must be a record keeper and announcement group. This club is the content provider and the CCTV of IIIT-K. Creative writing, photography, and videography competitions are organized to boost creative skills among the students. The club is for literature lovers and future authors and creative minds.

Tagline

"The Timekeeper"

Motto

"Socializing the institute by creating a domain of networks between students and the world around."

Agenda

  • Virtualizing the institute
  • Keeping a record of every event
  • Providing content to all clubs
  • Promoting institute and events by creating advertisements, posters etc.
  • Organizing intramural events and participating in extramural events

Working of the club

  • Keeping a record of all activities pertaining to the college.
  • Providing content (photos, videos, articles etc.).
  • Promoting and advertising the college on social media, websites etc. through posters, videos, photos etc.
  • Designing almanac, magazines, prospectus etc. of the college.
  • Organizing and participating in writing, photography and videography competitions in and out of the college.
  • Coordinating with other clubs and providing the contents for their events and competitions (like articles and kinds of stuff for debate etc.)
