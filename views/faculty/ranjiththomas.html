Background :

Formerly Senior Defence Scientist in DRDO (worked under Late Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam), experience spans over 30 years with Academia (worked as Principal and Head of the Department of CSE & IT of Engineering colleges) and Industry (as COO, CTO and Senior Consultant). Held positions as Editor, Technical Chair and Organizing Chair for several International Conferences. Actively interested in Research and Commercial Data Science projects.
