Background
Bini A.A graduated from Cochin University of Science and Technology in the year 2002 and did her Masters in Technology with specialization in Image Computing in 2005 from University of Kerala. She worked in Nest Technologies Trivandrum as an Embedded software developer during 2005-2007 and in KPIT Cummins Pune as Sr. Embedded system developer during 2007-2008. She pursued her Ph.D. during 2009-2015 in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, NITK Surathkal. After completing Ph.D. she worked as Associate Professor in St. Joseph Engineering college, Mangalore during 20015-2017. Since 2017 August She is working at IIIT Kottayam as Assistant Professor. Her areas of research interest include Signal and Image Processing, Data Compression and Communications
