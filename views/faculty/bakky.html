Research Interests

My broad field of research is nonlinear differential equations. Recently my work is focussed on dynamical systems governed by nonlinear fractional differential and differential - difference equations. The study of fractional differential equations (FDEs) has been handicapped due to the absence of well defined analytic techniques to deal with them in general and deriving their solution in particular. In my work, considerable efforts have been made to devise techniques to derive certain interesting mathematical aspects including solution of FDEs. My recent work extends the following analytic methods to FDEs :

  • Lie Symmetry Analysis
  • Invariant Subspace Method
  • Homotopy Analysis Method
  • Adomian Decomposition Method
