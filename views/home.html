IIIT Kottayam Welcomes You

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Kottayam at Valavoor, Pala, Kerala is one among the 20 new IIITs that have been proposed to be established across the country. The project is implemented on public-private partnership (PPP) mode with funding from the Central Government, State government and Private Partners.

The permanent campus for IIIT Kottayam is coming up at Valavoor, Pala, Kottayam, which is located in the southern part of Kerala, commonly referred to as Godâ€™s Own Country. Fifty three acres of land has been allocated for the project by the Government of Kerala. The construction and development of the campus has been planned in three phases. The first two phases, which include the basic academic, administrative and other developments, are estimated to cost Rs. 128 crore, which will be shared by the Central, State government and the industrial partners in the ratio of 50:35:15. The estimated cost of the third phase, which is envisaged as an expansion of the existing facility, will be around Rs. 70 crore.


The institution will offer B.Tech and B.Tech / MS , Ph.D courses in various engineering streams. It offers specialized courses covering all major research domains of computer science and engineering: Data science, Cloud Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Machine learning, Parallel Computing, HPC, Programming languages, Bio informatics, and so forth, as part of the 4 year B.Tech program. In addition, it offers Ph.D programs in CSE, ECE, and Mathematics.

The transit campus of the institution is presently functioning in the academic building of the Indian Institute of Science Education and Research , Thiruvananthapuram (IISER-TVM) at Gulati Institute of Finance and Taxation (GIFT) , Thiruvananthapuram and functioning under the ambit of the IIIT (PPP) Act 2017 under the mentorship of IISER-TVM . The permanent campus at Valavoor, Pala, Kottayam is expected to be functional in the Academic year 2018-19.
