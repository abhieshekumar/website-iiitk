Overview

All efforts are made to ensure a vibrant and creative student life at par with other Institutions of National Importance within the constrains of a transit campus. IIIT Kottayam ensures a 100 percent anti-ragging campus. The freshmen of the institute are treated without inequity and gender bias. IIIT Kottayam has several forums for students to organize and develop their skills. Clubs such as the Technical Club, the Sports Club, the Cultural Club, and the Social Club offer the students a platform to polish their skills.

The classes are conducted at Gulati Institute of Finance and Taxation, Thiruvananthapuram, C/o IISER-TVM. The permanent campus for IIIT Kottayam is coming up at Valavoor, Pala, Kottayam and the shifting of the institute to the permanent campus is expected to be in the academic year 2018-19. The permanent campus is envisioned to be well equipped with research labs based on the requirement of faculty/industries. Several research labs such as an IoT Cloud research lab, a Computer Networking lab, a 3D printing and design lab, etc. are to be looked forward to at IIIT Kottayam.

About IEEE

IEEE-IIITK is an IEEE initiation started at our institute to enrich the technical activities of our students/faculty. IEEE-IIITK is a proposed IEEE student branch section of IEEE Kerala Section, India. Currently, we have 22 student members and 2 faculty members registered at IEEE.

Hostel

Separate hostel accommodation is provided for boys and girls. There are two hostels for boys and one hostel for girls. All the hostels have common rooms, 24 hour Wifi-connectivity and recreational centres where newspapers, magazines and TVs are available. Round the clock water and electricity is provided. Laundry equipments are also available in the hostels. Dr. Vennapusa Sivaranjana Reddy is the Warden In Charge.

Medical Centre

Indian Institute of Science Education and Research, Thiruvananthapuram shares their medical centre facilities to IIIT-K students round the clock. A fully equipped ambulance handles emergency cases. Medical facilities are given vital importance and each student is given complete care.

IIIT Kottayam fraternity has celebrated International Yoga Day 2018. Around 60 students participated in Inter IIIT Sports Meet 2018 and won several prizes.

Internet

The transit campus is equipped with an internet band width of 30 Mbps with Wifi access. Internet connectivity is also extended to Boys' and Girls' hostels. There is no restriction on timing or limit for the internet usage. At present, there are 110 systems with wired connectivity and 10 access points are provided for wireless connectivity.
